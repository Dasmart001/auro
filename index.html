FAQ

Questions answered

What is AURO and how does it work?
AURO is a digital trading platform that deploys user capital across six high-yield trading markets — including crypto, forex, derivatives, prediction markets, and sports arbitrage. When you open a position, your capital is allocated to the corresponding market and you receive a fixed daily return for 60 days, which you claim manually each day.
How do I deposit funds?
Open the Deposit section in the app and enter your desired amount (minimum ₦5,000). Follow the payment instructions displayed on screen to complete the transfer. Once payment is confirmed by our team, your wallet balance is credited — typically within 1–3 hours during business hours.
How does the daily claim work?
Each position pays a fixed daily return for 60 days. You must log in and tap Claim each day — any day you miss is permanently forfeited with no compensation. Claims are credited to your wallet instantly and are withdrawable at any time. We recommend enabling daily reminders in your device settings.
How do I withdraw my earnings?
Go to the Withdraw page in the app and enter your amount and Nigerian bank account details. Minimum withdrawal is ₦15,000. All major Nigerian banks are supported. Withdrawals are processed manually — allow 1–3 business days from the time of submission.
What are the position limits?
The minimum single position is ₦5,000 on the Starter tier. The maximum is ₦1,000,000. You can hold multiple simultaneous positions across different tiers with no cap on total combined positions — as long as your wallet balance covers each one.
Can I open multiple positions at once?
Yes. You can hold positions across any combination of tiers simultaneously. Multiple positions in the same plan consolidate into a single claim card showing your combined daily return. Each day you claim once per plan, covering all positions in that tier together.
What are the risks involved?
All trading activities carry inherent risk. Returns are not guaranteed and past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results. You should only commit capital you can afford to lock in for the full 60-day position duration. By participating, you acknowledge and accept these risks as described in the Terms of Service.
How do I contact support?
Use the in-app support chat — open the app, tap your avatar, then select Customer Support. Choose your issue category and our team will respond directly in the chat. Support hours are Monday to Saturday, 9AM–8PM WAT. AURO will never ask for your PIN or password through any channel.
