FAQ

Questions answered

Is my money safe?
AURO deploys capital into real-sector businesses with physical assets — land, machinery, petroleum stocks, and gold. Your investment is backed by tangible operations. All investments carry inherent risk; we strongly advise investing only what you can afford to commit for the full 60-day period.
How does the daily claim work?
Each plan pays a fixed daily amount for 60 days. You must log in and tap Claim each day. Any day you miss is permanently forfeited — so we recommend enabling daily reminders in the app. Claims land in your wallet instantly and are withdrawable at any time.
How do I withdraw my earnings?
Go to the Withdraw page, enter your amount, select your bank, and provide your account details. Withdrawals are processed manually within 1–3 business days. You can withdraw to any Nigerian bank account — GTB, Access, OPay, PalmPay, Kuda, and more.
What's the minimum investment?
The minimum investment is ₦5,000 on the Starter tier. There is no maximum — our Sovereign tier goes up to ₦500,000 per slot, and you can hold multiple positions simultaneously across different tiers.
Can I invest in multiple plans at once?
Yes. You can run as many active investment positions as your wallet balance allows — across the same tier or different tiers. Multiple positions in the same plan automatically merge into one consolidated claim card so your dashboard stays clean.
How do I deposit funds?
Transfer your desired amount to AURO's designated OPay account shown on the deposit page, then submit your request in the app. Your wallet is credited within 1–3 hours after our team confirms the transfer. No card details required.
