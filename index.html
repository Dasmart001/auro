FAQ

Questions answered

Is my capital safe?
AURO deploys capital across six high-yield trading markets — crypto, forex, derivatives, prediction markets, and sports trading. All trading activities carry inherent risk and returns are not guaranteed. You should only commit capital you can afford to commit for the full 60-day duration. By participating, you acknowledge and accept these risks in full as described in our Terms of Service.
How does the daily claim work?
Each plan pays a fixed daily return for 60 days. You must log in and tap Claim every day — any day you miss is permanently forfeited with no compensation. We strongly recommend enabling daily reminders in your profile settings. Claims are credited to your wallet instantly and are withdrawable at any time.
What markets does AURO trade in?
AURO operates across six trading markets: micro-cap memecoin trading on decentralised exchanges, new crypto token launches and airdrops, leveraged cryptocurrency futures on major derivatives platforms, prediction markets, professional options and forex trading on major currency pairs, and systematic data-driven sports market arbitrage. Each tier is linked to a specific market.
How do I withdraw my earnings?
Go to the Withdraw page in the app, enter your amount and Nigerian bank account details. Withdrawals are processed manually within 1–3 business days. We support all major banks including GTB, Access, Zenith, UBA, OPay, PalmPay, and Kuda. Minimum withdrawal is ₦1,000.
What is the minimum and maximum trade?
The minimum trade is ₦5,000 on the Starter tier. The maximum single deposit is ₦1,000,000. You can hold multiple active trading positions simultaneously across different tiers, with no limit on your total combined positions on the platform.
Can I trade multiple plans at once?
Yes. You can hold as many active positions as your wallet balance allows across any combination of tiers. Multiple trades in the same plan automatically merge into one consolidated claim card showing your combined daily return — keeping your dashboard clean and easy to manage.
How do I deposit funds?
Select your deposit amount in the app (minimum ₦5,000), then transfer that exact amount to AURO's OPay account displayed on the payment page. Once transferred, tap "I Have Made This Payment" to notify our team. Your wallet is credited within 1–3 hours after confirmation. Do not submit a deposit request without actually completing the bank transfer.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our support team through the in-app live chat available in your profile settings, or by emailing support@auro.app. Support is available Monday to Saturday, 9am–8pm WAT. Response times are typically within 2–4 hours during business hours. For urgent deposit or withdrawal issues, use the in-app chat for fastest response.
